Step 1:
    Build minimum Viable Microwave
        - No power levels
        - No presets
        - No door sensor
        - No fancy display

Phase 1:
    You can press:
        Number buttons (0-9)
        Start
        Stop
        Clear

    Microwave does:
        Display time as you enter it
        Start countdown
        Stop when you hit Stop
        Reset when you hit Clear
        Ding when it reaches zero
